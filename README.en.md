# Privacy Policy — Avatar

**Last updated**: May 17, 2026
**Data controller**: Paripi ERP Co., Ltd. ("we", "us", "our")
**Application**: Avatar (アバター / 数字人 / 아바타)

---

## 1. Purpose

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, share, and protect information when you use the Avatar Android application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the information practices described here.

The App is a **B2B enterprise application**. It is operated as a kiosk terminal at facilities after registration (device code + password) by an organization administrator.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Automatically collected

| Type | Trigger | Purpose |
|------|---------|---------|
| **Voice data** | After "Start Session" tap, via microphone | Real-time AI conversation, speech recognition |
| **Camera frames** (optional) | Only when user explicitly enables the camera toggle | Visual assistance |
| **Device identifiers** | At first app launch | Device authentication, organization linkage |
| **App usage logs** | All feature usage | Bug fixing, service improvement |
| **Network info** | On WebSocket connection | Quality monitoring |
| **Coarse location** | At device boot (optional) | Device placement management |

### 2.2 Information you provide

- **Device code + password**: Issued by your organization administrator
- **Settings**: Language, voice, knowledge-base assignment

### 2.3 Information we DO NOT collect

- Visitor names, addresses, phone numbers, email addresses
- Credit card or financial information
- Biometric data (facial recognition, fingerprints)
- Contact lists, SMS, call logs

## 3. How We Use Information

Collected information is used only for:

- Core App functionality (AI conversation, digital human video streaming)
- Diagnosing and fixing issues
- Improving service quality and developing new features
- Detecting and preventing abuse
- Legal compliance (tax, consumer protection)

We **do not** use information for:
- Targeted advertising
- Sale to third parties
- User profiling

## 5. Data Retention

| Data type | Retention | Deletion method |
|----------|----------|----------------|
| Voice data (live session) | 7 days after session end | Automatic |
| Camera frames | Not stored server-side (real-time only) | N/A |
| Usage logs | 90 days | Automatic |
| Device credentials | Contract period + 12 months | Automatic on termination |

## 6. Security

- **In transit**: All communication uses TLS 1.2 or higher
- **At rest**: Server-side data encrypted with AES-256
- **Access control**: Only authenticated personnel
- **Incident response**: We notify affected customers and authorities within 72 hours of a material breach

## 7. Your Rights

You (or your organization administrator) have the right to:

- **Access** the personal information we hold
- **Rectify** inaccurate information
- **Erasure** ("right to be forgotten")
- **Data portability** in a machine-readable format
- **Object** to processing

Requests: **privacy@paripi-erp.com**
We respond within 30 days.

## 8. Camera and Microphone (Android Permissions)

The App uses the following Android permissions:

- **Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO)**: **Required** for AI conversation. Asked at app launch.
- **Camera (CAMERA)**: Optional. Activated only after explicit user tap and consent dialog. **Default OFF**.
- **Location (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION)**: For device placement management by the organization admin.
- **Bluetooth (BLUETOOTH_*)**: For integration with related hardware (chassis, etc.).

## 9. Children's Privacy

The App **is not intended for children under 13** (COPPA-compliant). We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 and will delete any such information promptly.
